January

January began with a wimper. After a fun holiday season, I finally got serious about striving to attain my goals mid-month. This blog post is the documentation of the latter.

After losing 30 pounds in 2012 and improving my overall health and diet by moving to a Paleo/slow-carb way of eating, I’d managed to let things slip and by mid-January had gained back 8lbs. The weight gain was no mystery, as I’d been drinking quite a bit of beer and had let my way of eating slip. As a measure of my fitness, I’d been running a familiar 10KM route throughout 2012 and had seen my time for that run decrease to below 50 minutes at it’s best. On January 13th, I ran that same route in 1:01:30.

Disappointed in myself, I decided to commit to run a minumum of 3 days per week. I also joined EverdayLegendary and began using the forum.
